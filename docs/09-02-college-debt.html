29  college-debt.csv

The data in college-debt.csv contains information for a random sample of \(n=250\) institutions of higher education in the United States. The attributes are:

  • institution: Name of the institution
  • city: City where the institution is located
  • state: State where the institution is located
  • type: Type of institution (Private; Public)
  • debt: Median amount of debt for a graduate of the institution
