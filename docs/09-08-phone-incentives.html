30  phone-incentives.csv

The data in phone-incentives.csv contains information for a sample of \(n=395\) participants enrolled in a incentive study. Participants were randomly assigned to an experimental group where they received a incentive or a control group.

The attributes are:

  • group: Whether the participant was a member of the experimental (Incentive) or control (Control) group
  • cooperate: Whether the participant cooperated (Yes) or not (No)
